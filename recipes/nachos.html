Nachos

Classic Mexican style chorizo nachos

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Chorizo
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • beans
  • Tomato
  • onion
  • avocado
  • sour cream
  • limes
  • salt
  • pickled jalapenos

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Grab a large baking sheet and set it aside. Shred the cheese and set it aside. Drain and rinse 1 can of beans and set that aside.
  2. Cook the chorizo, breaking it apart into small pieces as you go, in a medium skillet for 7-10 minutes (or until fully cooked through), flipping and turning it often.
  3. Add the tortilla chips directly to the baking sheet. Layer the nachos with a little cheese, a little chorizo, a little black beans and repeat until all of the ingredients are used up.
  4. Cube the tomato, and onions, then set aside. cut the avocado in half, then in a medium bowl smash till smooth. add the tomato and onion, then add lime and salt to taste.
  5. Bake for 5-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden brown.
  6. add the guacamole, sour cream, and pickled jalapenos to taste, then enjoy.
